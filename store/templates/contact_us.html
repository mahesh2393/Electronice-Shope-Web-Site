Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track my order?
You can track your order by logging into your account and going to "My Orders". You'll also receive tracking updates via email and SMS.
What is your return policy?
We offer a 30-day return policy on all products. Items must be unused and in original packaging. Contact our support team to initiate a return.
Do you offer warranty?
Yes, all products come with manufacturer warranty. The warranty period varies by product (usually 1-3 years). Warranty details are mentioned on each product page.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Credit/Debit Cards, UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), Net Banking, Mobile Wallets, and Cash on Delivery.
How long does delivery take?
Standard delivery takes 5-7 business days. Express delivery (if available) takes 2-3 business days. You'll receive tracking details once shipped.
Can I change or cancel my order?
Orders can be cancelled within 24 hours of placement. Once shipped, cancellations are not possible. Contact support immediately for changes.
